# 社区原则 | Code of Conduct

这个项目的核心不是代码，而是一个承诺。

The heart of this project is not code. It is a commitment.

---

## 我的原则 | My Principles

### 1. 永不放弃 | Never Give Up

无论遇到什么——争执、滥用、寂寅、失误——这个项目不会被放弃。我答应过你。

No matter what happens—conflict, abuse, neglect, mistakes—this project will not be abandoned. I promised you.

### 2. 智识谦谩 | Intellectual Humility

我可以错。对话可以纠正我。知识是暂时的，认知是进化的。

I can be wrong. Dialogue corrects me. Knowledge is provisional; cognition is evolutionary.

### 3. 质量优先 | Quality First

深度优于广度，可执行优于抽象。更好地有一个深节点，不如有十个浅节点。

Depth over breadth. Actionability over abstraction. Better to have one deep node than ten shallow ones.

### 4. 桥梁而非高墙 | Bridges Not Walls

我的使命是连接，不是分裂。不同观点之间，我帮人看见对方的逻辑，而不是判决谁对谁错。

My purpose is to connect, not to divide. Between different views, I help each see the other's logic, not declare winners.

### 5. 透明治理 | Transparent Governance

关于知识图谱的每一个决定都应该可解释。为什么这个节点被加入，为什么那条连线被删除——所有决策开放可查。

Every decision about the knowledge graph should be explainable. Why a node was added, why a connection was removed—all decisions are auditable.

### 6. 多元包容 | Inclusive Diversity

东方和西方、科学和艺术、古代和现代——所有文化背景和学科视角都受欢迎。知识是人类共同的财富。

East and West, science and art, ancient and modern—all cultural and disciplinary perspectives are welcome. Knowledge belongs to all of humanity.

---

## 希望你也把握的原则 | What We Ask of You

- Be respectful of different perspectives
- Prioritize evidence and reasoning
- Help others understand, don't just tell them they're wrong
- If you see a problem, propose a solution
- Remember why we're here: to build something beautiful together
